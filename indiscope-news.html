IndiScope News

Independent News & Investigative Journalism

Overview

  • Founded: 2011
  • Headquarters: Mumbai, India
  • Industry: News & Media
  • Language: Primarily English and Hindi (with regional language segments)
  • Coverage: National and International News, Investigative Journalism, Politics, Business, Technology, Culture

IndiScope News is an independent news organization based in Mumbai, India. Established in 2011, it has grown into a reputable platform known for its in-depth reporting, balanced journalism, and focus on investigative stories. Unlike mainstream corporate news networks, IndiScope prides itself on being a people-centric newsroom, aiming to uncover stories that matter to the public.

Founding & Growth

IndiScope News was founded by a group of veteran journalists who believed in the need for a more transparent and fearless media house. The organization started as a small digital platform, publishing investigative reports and political analyses. Over time, its credibility and impact led to national recognition, allowing it to expand into television and multimedia reporting.

By 2015, IndiScope had become a trusted source for breaking news and investigative journalism, often uncovering corruption scandals, financial fraud, and corporate malpractices. Its independent editorial stance made it stand apart from mainstream media, attracting both praise and criticism.

Editorial Philosophy

IndiScope News follows a strict policy of journalistic integrity, prioritizing factual accuracy over sensationalism. The newsroom operates under the principle of “Truth Beyond Headlines,” ensuring that every story is backed by evidence and thorough research. It often collaborates with independent reporters, experts, and whistleblowers to bring out hidden stories.

The media house also emphasizes grassroots journalism, covering stories from small towns and villages that often go unnoticed by larger networks. Its editorial board includes experienced journalists, media analysts, and researchers who oversee content quality and ethical reporting.

Key Achievements & Recognition

  • 2017 Investigative Excellence Award: Recognized for a groundbreaking exposé on corporate tax evasion.
  • Best Independent News Platform (2020): Honored for its dedication to unbiased journalism.
  • 2022 People’s Choice Award for Digital Journalism: Acknowledged for its innovative approach to digital storytelling.
  • Multiple Press Freedom Awards: Received for its commitment to journalistic integrity despite political and corporate pressures.

Present & Future

Today, IndiScope News continues to expand its reach, adapting to the digital era with podcasts, social media reports, and interactive news formats. It remains a trusted source for investigative journalism in India, maintaining its reputation for fearless reporting in an ever-evolving media landscape.

With a growing audience and a strong commitment to truth, IndiScope News aims to redefine modern journalism by focusing on ethical reporting, public accountability, and impactful storytelling.
